Description

This recipe has simple ingrediants but tons of flavor with a creamy ricotta mixture, savory meat sauce, and lots of mozerella cheese.

Ingrediants

  • Cheese filling
  • Neat Sauce
  • Lasagna Noodles/Chees Topping

Steps

  1. Spoon meat sauce on the bottom of a lightly greased cassrole dish
  2. Add 4 boiled lasagna noodles.
  3. Spread 1/3 of the ricotta cheese mixture on top.
  4. Add 1.5 cups of meat sauce.
  5. Repeat the same layers two more times.
