Tomato Soup

Home

Description

Tomato basil soup where it is important to offset the acidity with some key ingrediants. We use a combination of cream, parmesan, and a little sugar to balance the soup.

Ingredients

  • Butter
  • Yellow Onion
  • Garlic
  • Crushed Tomatoes
  • Chicken Stock
  • Basil
  • Sugar
  • Black pepper
  • Whipping cream
  • Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Saute Aromatics
  2. Make the tomato soup base
  3. Blend if desired
  4. Add cream and parmesan
  5. Serve
